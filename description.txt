Assorted Additions is a content mod which adds all kinds of things like the name implies. These are some of the additions:
- Around 200 items
- 13 NPCs
- 1 Boss
- New accessory category "Runes"

Also a bunch of tweaks such as:
- Hardmode ore progression changes
- Alternative ways to obtain some accessories
- Non consumable boss summon items
- Jungle Mimic in normal worlds with unique loot
- Lunatic Cultist boss loot
- Underground minibiome chests have unique loot

More details on the Workshop or GitHub pages.
